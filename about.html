Support

Our support team is here to help you with any issues or questions you may have. We offer a variety of ways to get in touch with us, and we strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I create an account? - You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the homepage.
  • How do I reset my password? - Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
  • How can I contact customer support? - You can contact us via email at support@gamingworld.com or by calling our customer support line at 1-800-GAME.
